# Slide 15 – Questions and Answers (Q&A)

## Talking Points

- Invite questions, comments, and real-world stories from teams adopting serverless on Kubernetes.
- Encourage sharing of operational lessons: monitoring, cost control, and team enablement.
- Capture follow-up topics for deeper dives or future sessions.

## Prompt Ideas

- “Which scaler or trigger would unblock your current backlog?”
- “What guardrails do you need from the platform team before you would run this in production?”
- “Where do you still feel a knowledge gap after today’s walkthrough?”
